Pecan Molassas Granola

An image of Pecan Molasses Granola

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsulfured molasses, not blackstrap
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • /2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp coarse salt
  • 3 cup rolled oats (not quick cooking)
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 cup pecans, roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together molasses, oil, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add oats, coconut, and pecans
  4. Stir gently with a rubber spatula until oats are completely coated
  5. Transfer to a rimmed baking sheet and spread in an even layer
  6. Bake, stirring occasionally, until oats are lightly browned, 25-30 min.
  7. Let cool on sheet on a wire rack, 30 min.
  8. Store in an airtight container at room temp. up to three weeks
